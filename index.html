NUS Overseas Colleges Silicon Valley: 1-Year Full-time Interns for Startups

The National University of Singapore (NUS) has been running an entrepreneurship internship program called NUS Overseas Colleges Silicon Valley for the past 17 years since 2002. It engages startups in SF Bay Area that are keen to host NUS undergraduate students for a 1-year internship. The interns come equipped with a 1-year internship visa and health insurance. Host companies have absolute discretion in deciding the intern’s scope of work. You will participate fully in the interview process to identify a suitable intern that will make a useful addition to any team within your company – business, marketing, operations, customer experience, data, engineering, product, design and others. The internship arrangement is non-binding, open and collaborative. The next batch of interns are scheduled to start work in January 2020 - till December 2020.
